# 12–16 Week Timeline

Phase 1: Discovery (Weeks 1–2)
- Secure domain, finalize branding & sitemap; roles assigned

Phase 2: Design (Weeks 3–5)
- 3 UI/UX mocks; hero/CTA; accessibility passes; 3 case study samples

Phase 3: Build (Weeks 6–9)
- Next.js web, Express API, Strapi CMS; integrate AI demos; pricing + quote form

Phase 4: QA & Compliance (Weeks 10–11)
- Performance & SEO setup; OWASP mitigations; GDPR/PDPA; QA report

Phase 5: Launch (Week 12)
- Deploy; GA + GSC; legal pages; announcement

Phase 6: Growth (Weeks 13–14+)
- Blog posts, A/B tests, SEO campaigns; monitor KPIs

KPIs: LCP ≤2.5s; INP ≤200ms; CLS ≤0.1; lead conversion ≥20%; bounce <40%
